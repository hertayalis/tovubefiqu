American Idol Contestant Matt Wilson Wiki | Bio

Matt Wilson (born on September 14, 2001) is a 21-year-old Buffalo-based singer who has been floating into the limelight in 2023 after participating in the ABC singing reality show “American Idol Season 21″ because in the audition round he captured the judges attention by sing “For Tonight” Song by Giveon.

All three judges loved his performance and said many nice things about his performance. Matthew is aware of his achievement because impressing Lionel, Katy, and Luke is a challenging task.

People on the internet are discussing Wilson, and some think that the Buffalo native will definitely reach the final round.

Do you know that Matt had shelved his dream of becoming a singer and was not hopeful of ever getting a chance to prove his talent? If your answer is ‘No,’ then we advise you to keep reading because this Celebsweek biography will tell you everything about this rising star.

How Old is Matt Wilson?

On September 14, 2001, Matthew, aka Matt Wilson (age 21), was born in Buffalo, New York, America. According to sources, his father, Jason Wilson, is an African American, and his mother, Lucita Morinello, is Puerto Rican. As WKBW said, Matt has always been one to dream big; he has been singing since he was 9 and used to dream about becoming a famous singer one day.

During school, he used to be part of the Football team, but he also used to spare some time for his music practice. In 2019 he graduated from high school and joined the Buffalo Academy of Visual and Performing Arts.

Eventually, he created a YouTube and started posting his music videos. Over the years, he uploaded countless short clips and 12 music videos. But sometimes, keeping the dream alive can be challenging. He stayed out of focus for some time and was about to give up his goal, but his wife Meli helped him realize that his dream wasn’t out of reach.

Who is Matt Wilson Wife?

Matthew is married, and his wife, Melisa Wilson, is his high school sweetheart. They have been together for a long time, and the singer considers Melisa his source of motivation.

We have also learned that it was Mel who motivated matt to return to singing, whereas the Buffalo guy was about to give up on his dream. If you want to know more about the American Idol contestant and his better half, follow him on Instagram (mattwilsonsings); the link is in the table.

Getting Back to the Pursuit of Dream

Fortunately, Matthew heard his wife and returned to pursuing his dream; not only did Matt stick with his goal, but he also ran with it and checked auditioning for season 21 of American idol off his bucket list.

Following his banging audition, Matt gave an interview to a local news channel and said, “It’s like a dream come true. You know, sometimes you think you’re prepared for something like that, but as soon as I stepped in to do that audition, man, it was like I’m actually in front of people with platinum records.”

Although Wilson received a lot of fame after appearing on American Idol, he has not forgotten where he comes from. In his words, “The sound that Matt Wilson is carrying, it’s coming from like a city that’s filled with love, it’s filled with so much culture and so many different ethnic backgrounds that led to his sound and led to the way that he sings James.”
