Zach Bryan tour ticket updates Ticketmaster general sale underway for I Remember Everything star

Publish date: 2024-05-17

Reach out to Tunespeak for assistance

Tunespeak is managing the Zach Bryan Presale registration.

A message on Ticketmaster on the presale read: "Tunespeak is managing the Zach Bryan Presale registration and code distribution for The Quittin Time Tour.

"For clarification on which show(s) you have access to, please reference the email you received from Zach Bryan Presale or reach out to registration@tunespeak.com for assistance."

Mixed reviews on presale

Some Zach Bryan fans lucked out and were able to get in the front of the queue to buy tickets at a fair price.

However, some are taking to X (formerly Twitter) to air their grievances after struggling to secure tickets.

One fan shared: "The bots are out in full force for the Zach Bryan Presale today."

However, another wrote: "shockingly got presale tix for Zach Bryan in Philly with ease."

A third added: "got zach bryan presale for ohio and toronto. life is good."

Queues moving along in DC

The queues for Washington DC are still open and moving along.

Prices are high in many cities

Fans are sharing that prices are too high to purchase a ticket in their city.

One concert-goer hopeful shared on X (formerly Twitter): "The cost of the tickets for the Zach Bryan presale in Toronto is criminal. How does anyone afford to go to a show right now?"

Another wrote: "The Zach Bryan presale prices are HEINOUS."

"I was second in the queue for the Zach Bryan presale but $322+ fees for a single floor ticket is insane," a third added.

Queues still open

Queues are still open to purchase tickets for Zach Bryan's tour.

There are over 2,000 people waiting to purchase tickets for the August 7 show in Philadelphia.

Tickets between $73 and $355+

Tickets are priced between $73 and over $400.

Lower bowl and floor seats are the most expensive seats, however, there are seats available in the upper bowl for less than $100.
