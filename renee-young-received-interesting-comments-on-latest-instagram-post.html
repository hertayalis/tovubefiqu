Renee Young Received Interesting Comments On Latest Instagram Post

Publish date: 2024-07-27

Renee Young is married to Jonathan Good, better known as Jon Moxley or Dean Ambrose or whatever you want to call him. The fact is that he is no longer a WWE Superstar and he obviously has his wife’s support.

Things could get awkward for Renee Young in WWE, but she is standing by her man’s decision and posted a picture on Instagram of the two together along with emojis of hearts and lightning bolts.

Sasha Banks replied to this post by saying: “All good things come to those who stay true.” Given her situation with WWE this is a very interesting response. However, she wasn’t the only one who commented.

Ryback commented with: “A worked shoot or like a shoot shoot? Either way looks like a hell of a shoot.”

AEW CBO Brandi Rhodes also replied by saying: “My jaw is on the floor I need Britt Baker to fix me.” This is humorous because Baker is a real dentist.

Other people such as Mickie James and the Bella Twins replied with emojis as well to show how much they love the post. It looks like Renee Young isn’t going to let anything stop how proud she is of her husband nor should she.
