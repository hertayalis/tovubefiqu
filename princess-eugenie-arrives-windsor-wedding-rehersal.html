Princess Eugenie Arrives at Windsor Castle Ahead of Her Wedding

Publish date: 2024-06-05

Princess Eugenie was just spotted arriving at Windsor Castle ahead of her royal wedding to Jack Brooksbank tomorrow. She and the groom-to-be were spotted driving in, and she couldn't look happier.

Sitting in the passenger's seat, Eugenie wore a black-and-blue paisley dress with a ruffled neckline, which royal watchers believe is by Claudie Pierlot. (She previously wore the look over the summer for a charity appearance.) The princess reportedly dyed her hair a deep cherry red ahead of the nuptials, but she kept her locks in a loose ponytail for the ride.

Her parents, Prince Andrew and Sarah, Duchess of York, and her sister Princess Beatrice were also spotted arriving.

Eugenie and Jack are expected to have a wedding rehearsal at Windsor today, which means we might be able to see other members of the royal family and the bridal party as they arrive.

The Palace just announced Eugenie's bridal party yesterday. As expected, her older sister Princess Beatrice will be the maid of honor, so we may see her arrive at Windsor for today's rehearsal as well.

The bridesmaids include Princess Charlotte and her cousins Savannah Phillips, Isla Phillips, and Mia Tindall, while Prince George is one of the page boys. So it's possible they and their parents—the Duke and Duchess of Cambridge, Peter and Autumn Phillips, and Zara and Mike Tindall—will make cameos for the rehearsal, too.

Similarly, Meghan Markle and Prince Harry were also seen arriving at Windsor two days before their wedding in May. Kate Middleton and Prince William were also spotted then, just weeks after welcoming Prince Louis.

Eugenie and Jack are expected to have 850 guests at their ceremony at Windsor Castle tomorrow. As royal weddings do, the ceremony will host members of the famous family and their celebrity friends, like Elton John, Ellie Goulding, and maybe even George and Amal Clooney.
