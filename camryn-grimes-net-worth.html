Camryn Grimes:

Camryn Grimes is an American actress. She started her career in the late ’90s, debuting in an episode of JAG. Shortly after, she got her first starring role as Cassie Newman on the soap opera The Young and the Restless. In 2001, she made her big screen debut in the film Swordfish. She also starred on the TV movie Sharing the Secret. At the same time, she had one-time roles on Medium, ER, Ghost Whisperer, Cold Case, NCIS: Los Angeles, and Mentalist. After her role as Cassie ended on The Young and the Restless in 2014, she secured the part of Mariah Copeland on the same show.
