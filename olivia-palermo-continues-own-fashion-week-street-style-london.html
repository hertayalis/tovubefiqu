See All of Olivia Palermo's London Fashion Week Outfits

Publish date: 2024-07-30

Fact: Olivia Palermo looks stylish no matter what city she's in or continent she's on. The style maven hopped the pond last week to take on London Fashion Week, and she did so in her signature effortlessly on-trend way. While Palermo stuck to just one chic ensemble a day during London Fashion Week, she upped the ante in London, wearing two or three outfits per day, and we're not complaining about the extra sartorial eye candy!

The street style star appropriately favored British designers while in London town, wearing everything from a colorful Matthew Williamson coat (which she styled as a dress) to a head-to-toe Burberry Prorsum fall/winter 2014 look complete with an ombré lace dress and chic belted scarf (above, left). (See how to style your scarf à la Burberry here!) Palermo also piled on the layers for the cooler London temps in coats, vests, and edgy leather on leather (above, right). We think it's safe to say she's the reigning champ of Fashion Month street style.
