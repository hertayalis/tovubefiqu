Katie Holmes Does Rock n Roll Glam in a Chanel Leather Jacket

Publish date: 2024-06-01

Katie Holmes brought the cool-girl energy to Chanel’s lavish Tribeca Film Festival event last night.

The actress and director was among the attendees at the fashion house’s annual Artists Dinner, which was held this Monday evening at New York City hot spot Balthazar. For the occasion, Holmes wore a black-and-white look that fully embodied her style: simple, sophisticated, and just the right amount of messy.

Gotham//Getty Images

The star of the head-to-toe Chanel ensemble was a black cropped leather jacket embellished with the brand’s signature golden chains at the pockets and sleeves, and crystal-encrusted golden buttons with the Chanel logo.

Lexie Moreland//Getty Images

The Rare Objects director wore it over a simple white tee and silky loose black high-waisted pants. She finished the outfit with glossy black leather shoes, two dainty gold necklaces, gold-and-diamond hoop earrings, and her nose hoop. She brought a little Chanel rock ’n’ roll with her messy, curly ’do and luxuriously chaotic glam: smoky eye makeup and a glossy pink nude lip.

Sean Zanni//Getty Images

Over the weekend, the Dawson’s Creek alum attended another Chanel event, the Tribeca Festival Women’s Lunch, and also wore chic staples by the brand. The look consisted of a black knit jacket with round silver buttons, layered over a white T-shirt with and paired with loose-fitting light-wash jeans, which were printed with faint white polka dots and the fashion house’s double CC logo. Other must-haves from the outfit included quilted black ballet flats adorned with the Chanel logo, a black quilted crossbody bag, layered gold necklaces, and a single dangling statement earring. Her hair was styled in soft waves and she wore a glossy berry lip.
