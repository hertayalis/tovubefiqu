Former stepmother Reba McEntire reveals what she really thinks of Kelly Clarkson

Publish date: 2024-06-27

The country superstar opened up about her longstanding relationship with the Since U Been Gone singer Kelly.

    Reba McEntire, the former stepmother of Kelly Clarkson’s ex-husband, Brandon Blackstock, recently expressed her immense pride in Kelly during her appearance on  The Voice. 

    The country superstar not only offered advice to the show's contestants but also opened up about her long-standing relationship with the Since U Been Gone singer. 

    Reba gushed: "I am so proud of Kelly. She has evolved as an artist and as a mentor. We've known each other since 2000 - actually, 2002 - she was just a baby then!" 

    She continued:  "Now, she has matured into this incredible woman that I am just so proud of, and I love her with all my heart."

    Kelly, visibly touched by Reba's words, embraced the 68-year-old legend and shared her own appreciation for their collaboration, saying: "I love working with Reba. She has helped me so much, both musically and as an artist."

    The two women share a unique bond, strengthened by their mutual love and respect despite Kelly's divorce from Brandon.

    During an appearance on Reba explained that she wouldn't choose sides between her former stepson and Kelly, as she loves them both.

    Reba added: "I've been friends with Kelly for a long time. And although Brandon is my stepson, I still love him like he's my blood, so I'm praying for them both because I love them both."

    The Voice coaches Niall Horan, Chance the Rapper, and Blake Shelton also chimed in on their experience working with the country icon. 

    Niall described Reba as "the absolute queen," praising her fun personality and extensive knowledge of singing. 

    Chance shared similar sentiments, appreciating the "amazing advice" Reba has provided to his team.

    Blake, who has a long history with Reba, reminisced about their friendship and professional collaboration, stating: "Having Reba here, it's just familiar territory for me because we've worked together a lot over the years."

    Reba reciprocated the warmth, reflecting on their enduring bond, saying: "Every time we're back together, it's like we haven't been apart."
