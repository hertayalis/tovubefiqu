Willy Cuh - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-07-17

Popular social media star who is best known for his .willycuh TikTok account where he posts comedy videos, often involving his friends. He has also posted a number of dances and lip-sync videos to songs by artists and bands like Katy Perry and Paramore. He has gained over 1.3 million followers on the platform. 

Before Fame

He launched his TikTok account in October 2019. 

Trivia

One of his most popular TikTok videos features him trying to pull a prank on a friend and it backfiring. In April of 2021, he posted a photo alongside El Guero MP on his Instagram and the post has received over 58 thousand hearts. 

Family Life

His real name is Guillermo Pena. His mom has appeared on his willycuh_ Instagram account. He's been based in Texas.

Associated With

He posted a duet with TikTok star AdamRayOkay in January 2020.
