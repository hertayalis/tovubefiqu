Ariel Winter Height, Age, Boyfriend, Biography, Family, Affairs &amp; More

Publish date: 2024-06-22

Ariel Winter (born January 28, 1998) is an American Actress, Model, Instagram Celeb and Social Media Personality from Fairfax, Virginia, United States. She is popular for playing the lead role as Alex Dunphy in the show titled “Modern Family“.

The actress is appearing on the famous show since the series start in 2009. Furthermore, the 21-year-old actress started her career with a minor role in the TV show “Listen Up!” as Little Girl in 2005. Furthermore, her real birth name is Ariel Winter Workman.

The same year she also made her silver screen debut Kiss Kiss Bang Bang as Young Harmony Faith Lane. Moreover, Ariel also gave her voice to the title character in the “Sofia the First” (Disney Junior show).

Additionally, the star also provided her voice of animated film “Mr. Peabody and Sherman” in 2014 as Penny Peterson. She also won 4 Screen Actors Guild Awards for Best Ensemble in a Comedy Series for Modern Family.

Profile, Parents & Before Fame

This beautiful Actress was born on the 28th of January 1998 in Fairfax, Virginia, United States. At present, Ariel Winter’s age is 21 years old. She is the younger daughter of Chrisoula (mother) and Glenn Workman (father).

Furthermore, Workman has two siblings i.e. an elder sister named Shanelle Workman Gray and brother Jimmy Workman. As of education, the artist homeschooled till the age of 14.

Personal Affairs, Boyfriend & Spouse

In 2011, Actress Ariel started dating her ex-boyfriend Callan McAuliffe (Australian actor). The pair remained together just only for a month. Later, Ariel had an affair with American actor Cameron Palatas from 2012 to 2013.

Furthermore, in 2014, she started a relationship with Laurent Claude Gaudette. They also appeared publicly at many events together including Coachella 2015. The former had an on and off affair until they finally quit in June 2016.

According to rumors, she also dated her Modern Family co-stars Nolan Gould. Currently, the actress is in a relationship with her partner Levi Meaden (Canadian Actor).
