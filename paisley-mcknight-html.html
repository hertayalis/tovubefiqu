Paisley McKnight - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-08-13

Second adopted child of Mindy and Shaun McKnight, the famous parents behind the YouTube channel Cute Girls Hairstyles. She was featured in the "Paisley GRADUATES from Elementary School | Behind the Braids Ep 145" video from June of 2021.

Before Fame

Her parents traveled across the United States to Pennsylvania in order to be with her when she was born. 

Trivia

She was first introduced in a video called "New Addition to Our Family" in March of 2011. Her mother has often done her hair in fancy braids. 

Family Life

Her siblings are Brooklyn, Bailey, Kamri Noel, Rylan and Daxton. 

Associated With

She and Brock Butler both gained fame as members of YouTube families.
