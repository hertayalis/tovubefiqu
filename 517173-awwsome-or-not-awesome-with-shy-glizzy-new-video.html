Awwsome Or Not Awesome With Shy Glizzy

Publish date: 2024-08-26

We had Shy Glizzy visit the NYC office the other week, and it seemed appropriate to ask the "Awwsome" rapper a bunch of "versus" questions, in which he would vote either 1) awesome or 2) not awesome. Following his responses, we hit the streets to ask random strangers the same questions and get their response on whether said things were awesome or not awesome. The questions ranged from Kim Kardashian's recent naked GQ photo shoot to beards to the ALS Ice Bucket Challenge to Gucci Mane.
