Popular Tiktoker Ahoufe is dead

Publish date: 2024-08-19

According to confirmed reports reaching the front desk of GHBase.Com, popular Ghanaian Tiktoker Ahoufe has died.

According to accounts, the Tiktoker died after a long battle with an unknown illness.

Ahoufe’s looks and comic nature in some of the videos he posts on social media helped him go viral.

Not only that, but his image became one of the most popular stickers on some social media platforms in the nation, demonstrating his popularity.

After learning of his death a few minutes ago, his admirers began flooding his timeline with condolence messages for his family.
